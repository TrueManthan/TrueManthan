ABOUT TrueManthan

TrueManthan is an informative and educational platform that delves into the financial, technical, mental, social, and spiritual aspects of the education system. The channel investigates the truth behind various issues and presents in-depth, analytical video, blogs, & varieties of training and workshop's.The primary aim of the channel is to create awareness, encourage critical thinking, and bring forth the reality behind pressing issues in education and society. TrueManthan is a value-centric platform committed to balancing human values, truth observation, right understanding, family relationships, and harmony in material needs, ultimately aiming for holistic development at the personal, family, societal, and natural levels.



How does TrueManthan Work?

Topic Selection
(Listening to the Voice of Society)

TrueManthan starts by listening. Topics are selected through: Citizens' complaints, current events, and local issues Online polls and public suggestion forms Consultations with educators, social activists, and students



In-depth research
( Looking Beyond Headlines )

Once a topic is finalized, the research includes:Government Resolutions (GRs), laws, and policy documents Information obtained via RTI, audit reports, court rulings Historical context and expert analysis First-hand accounts and documented experiences All content is cross-verified to eliminate misinformation and fake news.



Creation of Visual Media
( Making Information Accessible to All )

Complex information is simplified and presented through: Animated videos and visual explainers Voiceovers and subtitles in regional languages Charts, infographics, maps, and timelines Case studies and dramatized re-enactments



Public Dialogue and Participation
( Building a Knowledge Network )

Every voice matters at TrueManthan. Engagement happens via: Live YouTube sessions and interactive webinars Feedback forms, surveys, and group discussions Programs in colleges, schools, and social institutions Open invitations for volunteers, researchers, and local voices



Collective Solutions and Outreach
From Awareness to Action

The final step involves suggesting and initiating solutions: Reports based on research, dialogue, and public input Submission of representations to government bodies, courts, or universities Dissemination through digital platforms to gather public support Follow-up content to track responses or inaction





What We offer?

1. Educational analysis

Education is the foundation of a progressive society. TrueManthan conducts objective and analytical evaluations of schools, colleges, and private educational bodies. We assess their policies, functioning, quality standards, and assessment methods. Our goal is to present clear, understandable insights to parents, students, and educators about institutional strengths, weaknesses, and improvement opportunities.



2. Mental and social awareness

Mental health issues like depression, stress, and peer pressure among students are growing concerns. TrueManthan works to raise awareness about emotional well-being, effective family communication, and social inequalities. We believe that emotional balance and social consciousness are essential for developing strong, capable individuals.



3. Technical awareness

Being academically good is not enough anymore. To thrive in today‚Äôs world, students must develop essential life skills like communication, decision-making, teamwork, problem-solving, digital literacy, and creative thinking. In parallel, technical proficiency is a must to stay relevant in a fast-evolving job market.



4. Spiritual and moral perspectives

Through TrueManthan, we provide youth and students with practical knowledge in modern technologies like AI, coding, data analysis, and cybersecurity. We also organize skill-building workshops, seminars, and training programs to enhance their life skills. Our mission is to empower individuals not just for jobs but for life itself.

5. Issue Identification

Alongside academic and technical knowledge, spiritual insight and moral values are vital for a fulfilling life. We promote practices such as yoga, meditation, Indian philosophy, introspection, and ethics to help individuals develop a holistic life approach. A values-based perspective is essential for both personal and societal growth.
