UNIVERSAL HUMAN VALUES (UHV)

Great people have tried to live with truth, love, and compassion. They understood themselves, the harmony in nature, and the human role in existence. They worked to create a just society for the well-being of all. We feel deeply grateful to such great individuals.

Universal Human Values (UHV) is a path of self-exploration for human well-being. It is an initiative to integrate human values into education, enabling students to discover values through thoughtful self-exploration and natural acceptance, rather than imposed rules.

Objective: To facilitate value-based personal transformation and the creation of a just, compassionate society.

The content is not a set of do’s and don’ts, but a set of proposals for self-verification. It is universal, logical, experiential, and aligned with human welfare. Through this approach, students explore the inherent harmony and coexistence in existence, leading to the development of ethical competence and value-based decision-making in life.

The larger goal is to awaken human consciousness at the individual level and nurture a harmonious, humane society.

  • Experiential, inquiry-based learning
  • Universal, rational, and human-centric content
  • Rooted in Natural Acceptance, leading to self-confidence, Evolution of Right Understanding, Understanding of Relationship
  • Focus on inner ethical competence, not external enforcement
  • Understanding harmony at all levels — individual, family, society, and nature
