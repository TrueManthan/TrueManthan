ABOUT TrueManthan

TrueManthan is an informative and educational platform that delves into the financial, technical, mental, social, and spiritual aspects of the education system. The channel investigates the truth behind various issues and presents in-depth, analytical video, blogs, & varieties of training and workshop's.The primary aim of the channel is to create awareness, encourage critical thinking, and bring forth the reality behind pressing issues in education and society. TrueManthan is a value-centric platform committed to balancing human values, truth observation, right understanding, family relationships, and harmony in material needs, ultimately aiming for holistic development at the personal, family, societal, and natural levels.
